GENERAL OVERVIEW

The University of Ghana uses a holistic approach to admissions, considering factors such as academic achievement, standardized test scores, extracurricular activities, and personal statements.

•Admission Decisions

Admission decisions are typically made within six to eight weeks of the application deadline. Applicants will be notified of their admission status via email and can check their application status on the UG Admissions Portal.

•Additional Information

For more information on University of Ghana admissions, please visit the university's website or contact the Admissions Office.

•Application Deadlines

Undergraduate applications typically open in October and close in February for the next academic year. Postgraduate applications open in November and close in March for the next academic year.

Undergraduates

⬤ Admission Requirements

⬧ Undergraduate Admissions

• Applicants must have completed secondary education with a minimum of six West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) or Ghana Senior High School Certificate Examination (SSSCE) credits, including English and Mathematics, with grades of C6 or better.

• Applicants must also have obtained satisfactory scores in the University of Ghana Basic Education Certificate Examination (UG BECE) or its equivalent.

• International applicants must meet equivalent requirements from their respective countries.

⬤ Application Process

⬧ Undergraduate Admissions

• Applicants must create an account on the UG Admissions Portal and complete the online application form.

• Applicants must upload scanned copies of their academic transcripts, certificates, and other supporting documents.

• Applicants must pay the application fee.

Postgraduates

⬤ Admission Requirements

⬧ Postgraduate Admissions

• Applicants must have a bachelor's degree from a recognized university with a minimum grade of Second Class Upper Division (2.1).

• Applicants must also have obtained satisfactory scores in the relevant postgraduate entrance examinations.

• International applicants must meet equivalent requirements from their respective countries.

⬤ Application Process

⬧ Postgraduate Admissions

• Applicants must upload scanned copies of their academic transcripts, certificates, and other supporting documents.

• Applicants must also upload scanned copies of their research proposals.

• Applicants must create an account on the UG Admissions Portal and complete the online application form.

• Applicants must pay the application fee.
